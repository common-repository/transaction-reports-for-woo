Generates a report on products sold on date wise, category wise, customer wise, etc. with CSV download option and send to any email.

== Description ==

Increase your sales with insight from reports generated on basis of various conditions to observe the sales on basis of products, date, customers, charges, fields, etc. 
 

== Installation ==

* Upload the Transaction reports for WooCommerce plugin to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
* Activate the Transaction reports for WooCommerce plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress


== Frequently Asked Questions ==

= Where is plugin menu? =
* Go to WooCommerce menu > Product Transactions

= What options I get in this plugin? =
* Get sales report on basis of date, week, month, year, etc.
* Get sales report on basis of date range.
* Get sales report on basis of required fields of a product like product name, total sold, gross sales, SKU, etc.
* Compare various kind of user roles, customers, guests, customer list and sales by them.
* Get only tagged products list, only products with field, categories and many other options.
* Get sales information on basis of different order status.
* You get output in easily understandable form.
* Download csv, html, etc formats available for report or email it to any email address.
